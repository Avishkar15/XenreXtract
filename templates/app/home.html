About XenreXtract

Hey there! I'm Avishkar, a 22-year-old junior UX designer who has always had a penchant for liking every song that catches my ear on Spotify. However, I soon found myself facing a dilemma - how could I efficiently organize these songs into playlists based on specific genres?

So, I decided to put my creativity to work and developed an app to solve this problem. Now, with XenreXtract , you can effortlessly turn your liked songs into playlists based on your favorite genres.

As a music enthusiast and UX designer, my goal is to simplify the process of curating personalized playlists, allowing users to effortlessly enjoy their favorite genres. I strive to continuously innovate and enhance the music listening experience for everyone.
